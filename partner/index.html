Partners

Umpqua Bank is a full service bank that strives to create a unique and memorable banking environment in which our customers perceive the bank as an indispensable partner in achieving their financial goals.

Studio L’Image is a graphic design studio specializing in Visual Communication and User Information Design. Our primary disciplines include Identity & Branding, Architectural Signage and Graphics, and Interpretive Design. Studio L’Image was incorporated in 1983 in California and maintains offices in San Francisco and Sebastopol, CA and New York, NY.

Friendly service and world class customer support are the hallmarks of Sebastopol Mac Daddy. From a tiny shop in downtown Sebastopol, they provide a full suite of repair services and diagnostics for all Apple products - from screen repairs and water damage on iPads, iPhones and iPods, to hard drive and RAM upgrades for iMacs, MacBooks and MacBook Pros, just to name a few, as well as training and upgrades for all Apple devices.

Located just across the road from Rite Aid, Mac Daddy is easily accessible for all of West County's Apple users. Save a trip to Santa Rosa or Rohnert Park, and come to Mac Daddy's Headquarters or have them come to you - they do house calls too!

We at SEP recognize that not all of our local businesses are in a position to financially contribute directly to our organization. As the economic impact of investments by our SEP clients in the goods and services they need to build their businesses demonstrates (more than $230,000 in SEP’s most recently ended fiscal year), SEP clients prefer to invest locally to meet their needs whenever possible.

Many local businesses are becoming Sebastopol Entrepreneurs’ Project Partners and extending their support to SEP clients while growing their own businesses. Whether you are a printer, a web developer, a bookkeeper or accountant, a lawyer, run a commercial kitchen, or provide any of the myriad goods and services businesses depend on, you can help SEP clients get started and, at the same time, build your own stable of dedicated, loyal customers.

SEP Partners generally provide our clients an initial discount or introductory offer on the goods and services they offer. They are included on the Partner Listing on the SEP website and are recognized at SEP events and programs. Please contact Gay McFarren, our Executive Director (gmcfarren@sepyes.com) to discuss becoming a SEP Partner.
