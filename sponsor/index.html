Sponsors

You are invited to become a financial sponsor for one of the most dynamic engines of economic growth in West Sonoma County.

The goals of the Sebastopol Entrepreneurs’ Project (SEP) continue to focus on job creation and economic growth for West Sonoma County. Our business advising program, low-cost co-work space, along with the SEP business education program provide support and guidance for start-up and growing businesses in our community. The achievements and successes of our SEP clients contribute economic impact for our region – not only through the jobs these businesses create or save, but also through the direct investments they make in the business community.

During 2012 the latest full-year data available, SEP clients independently reported combined revenues in excess of $420,000. Revenue for SEP clients grew to nearly $300,000 during the first half of 2013, creating or saving 13 full-time and 2 part-time jobs and SEP Clients collectively during this period spent more than $230,000 in Sonoma County on supplies and services.

SEP depends on the generous support of our sponsors to enable us to provide the support and guidance our clients need to grow and compete in the challenging business climate that we are in.

Our corporate and private sponsors generously share their success with SEP thereby enabling us to guide the next generation of west Sonoma County business leaders. Please contact Ludwig Furtner, President, SEP Board of Directors; Gay McFarren, Executive Director; or any of our Board members to discuss your contribution.

SEP is classified a 501(c) (3) Public Charity by the Internal Revenue Service. Appropriate documentation will be provided to financial sponsors.
