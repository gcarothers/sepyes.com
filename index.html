Sebastopol Entrepreneurs Project

Helping Businesses Start, Learn, Grow, Thrive!

The mission of the Sebastopol Entrepreneurs Project is to support local, sustainable businesses by providing low cost co-work space, advisement and education, services.

Advising

SEP offers individual advising and consulting sessions starting with two no-cost meetings so you can determine if SEP is right for you. Whether you are just starting out or are already in business SEP can help.

Apply for advising

Co-working

SEP offers low-cost office and shared workspace, high-speed Internet access, a conference room, on site office equipment, a business mailing address for startup companies and a convenient kitchenette. These amenities can be rented by the day, week or month.

Sign up for space

Education

SEP offers free public seminars on subjects that range from marketing and selling, finance and accounting, legal and technology issues and how to thrive under the pressure. Give us your e-mail and we’ll keep you posted on dates, times and topics. From initial concept and bringing your product to market, to growing an existing company SEP can help you.

Sponsors & Partners

The goals of the Sebastopol Entrepreneurs’ Project (SEP) continue to focus on job creation and economic growth for west West Sonoma County. Our business advising program, low-cost co-work space, along with the SEP business education program provide support and guidance for start-up and growing businesses in our community. The achievements and successes of our SEP clients contribute economic impact for our region – not only through the jobs these businesses create or save, but also through the direct investments they make in the business community.
